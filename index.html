About me

Antoine A. Ruzette

I strive for research so beautiful it is mistaken for art, and art so technical it is mistaken for science.

My formal education is Bioengineering, Bioinformatics and Computer Science. My research has spanned: machine learning for bacterial genome mining, bioimage analysis and computer vision for microscopy image analysis, simulation of embryonic development in 3D, and medical device development for remote tissue evaluation. I joined Harvard University as an Associate in the Department of Systems Biology in 2023.

Favorite Tools:

  • Python
  • Blender
  • QuPath
  • Libraries I often use: numpy, scipy, open-cv, bpy and open3D
